Traditional Rwandan culture.


Culture of Rwanda - history, people, traditions, women ...
With its long history of hierarchical social relations,
Rwandan culture puts great emphasis on practices
of etiquette that demonstrate respect and emphasize social rank both inside and outside the family.
Within the family, chairs are traditionally reserved for men,
while other family members sit on mats on the floor.
